## Batch Tips for Midjourney

1. **Run all prompts at `--q 2`** for highest quality
2. **Use `--s 500`** for a balance of Midjourney style and photorealism
3. **Add `--v 6.1`** (or latest version) at the end of each prompt if needed
4. **Upscale your favorites** to at least 2048px wide before using on the site
5. **Export as WebP or optimized JPEG** (quality 80) and host on your CDN or in `/public/images/`
6. After generating, place images in `/public/images/` and I'll update all the `src` references across the codebase
